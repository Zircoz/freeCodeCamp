Here are the appearences of Jacque in books and periodicals

  • 2010: Contributing author to the book Explore All Possibilities by Jack Frank
  • 2004: Feature contributing author to the book In the Shadow of War edited by Arthur Shostak Ph.D.
  • 2003: Feature contributing author to the book Viable Utopian Ideas: Shaping A Better World edited by Arthur Shostak Ph.D.
  • 2003: Wrote the preface and was featured in the book Transforming The Global Biosphere: Twelve Futuristic Strategies by Elliott Maynard, Ph. D.
  • 2002:Feature article, Engineering a New Vision of Tomorrow in the 2002 January - February issue of The Futurist.
  • 2001: Feature cintributing author in Utopian Thinking in Sociology: Creating the Good Society, an instructional book published by American Sociological Association.
  • 1994: Feature article, Designing the Future: A Cybernatic City for the Good Society in the 1994 May-June issue of the World Future Society's magazine, The Futurist, where the editors compared his work with R. Buckminster Fuller and Paolo Soleri.
